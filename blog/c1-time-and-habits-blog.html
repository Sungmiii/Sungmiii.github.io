C1-Time and Habit

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

'Pomodoro Technique'&'How to Create a Habit' and 'The Power of Meditation' those three I've tried before.

What did you find interesting?

I actually surprise that 'Pomodoro Technique' is working, because it's never worked me at all. I am not able to concentrate if there is the time limit at the each section, I prefer to make a time line each day and it makes me comfortable.

what worked for you?

'How to Create a Habit' it's worked for me mostly. I tried to wake up at the same time everyday and starting study at the similar time. However if I wake up late and didn't start studying at the similar time, feel really guilty and nervous.

Which will you experiment further with?

I should try'The Power of Meditation' to calm down when I feel very nervous and guilty. Maybe try a yoga with.
